Chilli and rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin of kidney beans
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Oil
  • 1/2 cup of rice

Instructions

  1. Boil water for rice
  2. Chop the onion and crush or grate the garlic
  3. Put the onion in a pan with a little oil and cook until clear
  4. Add the garlic and add herbs and spices
  5. Add the tin of tomatoes and kidney beans and pour water into pot with rice cook
  6. Cook rice and chilli for 20 minutes
  7. Drain the rice and mix with the chilli
