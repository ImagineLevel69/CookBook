Jacket potato

Ingredients:

  • 1 tin of baked beans
  • 2 potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C on grill
  2. Pierce skin of potatoes several times with a fork
  3. Microwave the potatoes for 4 minutes, flip them over. Microwave for another 4 minutes
  4. Put potatoes on baking tray and cover in oil
  5. Grill potatoes for 10 minutes
  6. Microwave baked beans for 2 minutes, stir and microwave for another 1 minute
  7. Cut open potatoes, pour in beans and grate cheese on top
