Jacket potato

Ingredients:

  • 1 large potato
  • Butter
  • Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F)
  2. Wash the potato and prick it with a fork
  3. Bake the potato in the oven for 1 hour or until tender
  4. Cut the potato open and add butter and cheese if desired
