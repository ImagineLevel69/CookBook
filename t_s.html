Tomato sauce and pasta

  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • oil
  • pasta (75g is a portion but you can just eyeball it)

Instructions

  1. Boil water for pasta
  2. Chop the onion and crush or grate the garlic
  3. Put the onion in a pan with a little oil and cook until clear
  4. Add the garlic and add herbs
  5. Add the tin of tomatoes and pour water into pot with pasta and a little oil cook for 10 minutes
  6. Drain the pasta and mix with the sauce
